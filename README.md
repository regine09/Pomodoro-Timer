# Pomodoro-Timer

Introduction
The POMODORO Timer Kit is an ESP32-based smart time management device created to help students improve their study habits using the Pomodoro Technique. This technique divides work into structured intervals of focused activity followed by short breaks, allowing users to maintain concentration while preventing mental exhaustion.

The project was developed in response to common challenges experienced by students, such as procrastination, difficulty maintaining attention, and inefficient study routines. As digital devices become essential tools for learning, they also introduce distractions that can interrupt focus and reduce productivity.

To address this issue, the POMODORO Timer Kit provides a dedicated physical timer that guides users through structured work sessions and break periods. The device combines simple hardware components—including an ESP32 microcontroller, OLED display, buttons, and a buzzer—with an easy-to-use interface to support effective time management during study sessions.

Importance
The POMODORO Timer Kit serves as a practical tool that helps students structure their study sessions in a consistent and manageable way. By guiding users through timed focus periods and scheduled breaks, the device encourages discipline and promotes a more organized approach to studying.

In addition to supporting productivity, the kit also introduces learners to basic concepts in electronics and embedded systems. Since the device can be assembled by users, it provides an opportunity for hands-on learning that connects theoretical knowledge with real-world technology applications.
